Instructions

A rhetorical question(RQ) is a question someone asks without expecting an answer. The question might not have an answer, or it might have an obvious answer.

People use RQ to achieve a pragmatic goal, such as structuring an argument, being persuasive, emphasizing a point, or being ironic.
